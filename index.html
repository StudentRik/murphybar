About

The Murphy Bar can be used in the house or outside as a bar for your garden.

Many people have chosen to put the Murphy bar in their kitchen to use as a kitchen table and cupboard. This is ideal for those who have only a small area in which they can put a kitchen table.

The original use of the Murphy bar was to be used as a bar, this can be in your garden, outhouse, garden room or even a mancave!. Either way the bar is practical and a useful addition to any home or garden.
